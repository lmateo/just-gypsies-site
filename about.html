About US

We invite you to visit Just Gypsies Farm, an equine facility for year-round riding, located in North Newport, Maine. Our beautiful 160 acre farm offers trails, indoor and outdoor arenas, round pen, cross country field, and a water complex. Contact us to arrange a tour of our facilities, discuss our offerings and enjoy the serene setting of Just Gypsies Farm.
