Choose the right class for you

We offer 1-to-1 private tuition for riders of all levels. We start beginners with half-hour private lessons while they learn the basics and build their confidence. The more experienced riders will benefit from personal tuition too.

Weekly riding lessons are held from March to October. These lessons are the ideal way to learn to ride. Our horses are well schooled and weekly lessons are the more affordable way to learn to ride than owning a horse

Advanced horse riding lessons, unlike beginner and intermediate horse riding lessons, have an intense focus on how the rider affects the horse’s way of going. For this reason, riders taking advanced riding lessons have to have exquisite balance, body control, and a complete understanding of how the horse’s body works.
