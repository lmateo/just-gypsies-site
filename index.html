About Gypsy Vanner Breed

The Irish Cob, also known as the Gypsy Cob, Gypsy Horse, ‘’’Irish Tinker’’’, or Gypsy Vanner, is a type or breed of domestic horse from the British Isles. It is a small, solidly-built horse of cob conformation and is often, but not always, piebald or skewbald; it is particularly associated with the Pavee and Romani travelling peoples of Britain and Ireland.
